Biblical Events Timeline

The Torah (Old Testament) is represented by the blue nodes, and the Bible (New Testament) is represented by the orange nodes.


You may use your mousepad zoom in and out of the graph, or you can do so by dragging the bar below.
You can also click on a node in order to show a detailed description of the event and see an image relating to it.
Hovering over a node also gives you additional information such as the specific year of the event, and which book(s) it is mentioned in.
