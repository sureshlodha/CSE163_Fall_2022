Endangered Languages

Human language is a uniquely diverse and complex form of culture, but this unique catalog of human experience shrinks everyday from sources as hostile as outright genocide to the apathetic motions of urbanization. Often times people take for granted the linguistic diversity that the world holds and could potentially lose over the next one hundred years, by learning about the inherent heterogeneity of human languages and their closeness to extinction in just the languages that have been recorded, we can observe the dire need for conservation in both documentation and active preservation. Without intervention many of these languages will be lost forever.
